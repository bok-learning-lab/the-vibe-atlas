---
title: the atlas
description: vibe coding tools arranged by level of abstraction from the ground truth of string in, string out. the further down you go, the more the tool handles for you -- and the less direct control you have.
---

at every tier, the same thing is happening underneath. a string goes into a model, and a string comes out. the question is how many layers sit between you and that exchange -- and whether, for what you're building, those layers help or hide.
